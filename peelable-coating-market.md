<p><p>Peelable coatings are protective layers that can be removed easily after serving their purpose. The market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Solvent-Based Coatings** - These contain organic solvents, offering strong adhesion and durability but may raise environmental concerns.</p><p>2. **Water-Based Coatings** - These are more eco-friendly, using water as a solvent, providing easy application and clean-up while maintaining performance.</p><p>3. **Hot-Melt Coatings** - These are applied in a molten state, offering quick application and robust adhesion, ideal for various manufacturing processes. Each type caters to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
