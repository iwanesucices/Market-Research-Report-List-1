<p><p>The Explosives Trace Detection market encompasses two main technologies: Body Detection Technology and Trace Detection Technology. Body Detection Technology focuses on identifying explosives on individuals, typically using advanced sensor systems that can scan clothing or surfaces. Trace Detection Technology, on the other hand, involves detecting minute particles of explosives present in the environment, often utilizing methods such as swabbing and chemical analysis. Both technologies are critical for enhancing security and ensuring safety in high-risk areas like airports and public events.</p></p>
