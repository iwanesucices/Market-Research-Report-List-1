<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid surface countertops are crafted using two primary molding techniques: press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing a mixture of acrylic or polyester resins with fillers under high pressure to create durable, seamless panels. This method is efficient and allows for consistent thickness and surface quality. In contrast, casting molding involves pouring a liquid resin mixture into molds, allowing for intricate shapes and designs. Both techniques contribute to the versatility, aesthetic appeal, and functionality of solid surface countertops in various applications.</p></p>
