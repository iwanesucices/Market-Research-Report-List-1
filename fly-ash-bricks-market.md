<p><p>The fly ash bricks market is segmented into Class F and Class C fly ash bricks. Class F fly ash bricks are produced using fly ash from coal combustion, offering high strength and durability, and are often used in non-load bearing applications. Class C fly ash bricks contain calcium-rich fly ash, providing higher compressive strength and hydration properties, making them suitable for structural applications. Both types are eco-friendly, lightweight, and contribute to sustainable construction practices, catering to diverse building needs.</p></p>
