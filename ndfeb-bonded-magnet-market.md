<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Pressing Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NdFeB bonded magnet market is primarily segmented into two types based on manufacturing processes: injection molding and pressing molding. Injection molding involves mixing NdFeB powder with a polymer binder and injecting it into molds, allowing for complex shapes and high production rates. Pressing molding, on the other hand, involves compacting the powder into a mold under pressure, followed by sintering. This method typically provides superior magnetic performance and mechanical properties, suited for various applications across industries like automotive and electronics.</p></p>
