<p><p>The fever thermometer market is categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact thermometers use infrared technology to gauge body temperature from a distance, providing quick readings without physical contact, making them ideal for clinical settings or public health use. In contrast, contact thermometers require placement against the skin for an accurate measurement and typically include oral, rectal, or axillary types, often used for home monitoring. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
