<p><p>The Dental CAD & CAM materials market encompasses various types, each playing a crucial role in restorative dentistry. Glass ceramics are known for their aesthetic qualities and translucency, ideal for esthetic restorations. Alumina-based ceramics offer high strength and durability. Lithium disilicate, with its excellent mechanical properties, is popular for veneers and crowns. Zirconia is favored for its exceptional strength and biocompatibility, suitable for bridges and implant frameworks. Other materials may include composites and resin-based options, expanding the range of restorative possibilities.</p></p>
