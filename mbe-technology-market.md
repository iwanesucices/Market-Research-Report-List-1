<p><ul><li>Normal MBE Systems</li><li>Laser MBE Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MBE (Molecular Beam Epitaxy) technology encompasses two primary market types: Normal MBE Systems and Laser MBE Systems. Normal MBE Systems rely on traditional effusion cells to produce thin films with precise atomic layers, widely used in semiconductor fabrication and material research. In contrast, Laser MBE Systems incorporate laser technology to enhance control and efficiency in layer deposition, allowing for improved precision and greater versatility in material applications. Both systems cater to advanced industries, including electronics and optoelectronics.</p></p>
