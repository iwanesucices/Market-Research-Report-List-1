<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Two wheeler Keyless Entry System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler keyless entry system market comprises two main types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows users to unlock their vehicles automatically upon proximity detection without pressing buttons, enhancing convenience and security. In contrast, RKES requires users to press a button on a remote to unlock the two-wheeler, providing ease of access but necessitating manual action. Both systems aim to improve user experience while offering advanced security features for modern two-wheelers.</p></p>
