<p><strong>The Guided Airflow System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mixing Ventilation</li><li>Parallel Flow Ventilation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Guided Airflow System market comprises two main types: Mixing Ventilation and Parallel Flow Ventilation. Mixing Ventilation involves the blending of supply and return air to achieve uniform temperature and air quality throughout a space, often utilized in commercial buildings. In contrast, Parallel Flow Ventilation directs airflow in a more stratified manner, minimizing mixing to maintain specific temperature zones, commonly used in areas requiring precise climate control. Both systems aim to enhance indoor air quality and energy efficiency.</p></p>
