In contrast, multiple-layer films combine several graphite layers, enhancing thermal conductivity and durability, suitable for high-performance devices needing robust heat dissipation. Both types cater to different industry needs, such as electronics, automotive, and renewable energy, driving advancements in thermal management technologies.</p></p>
