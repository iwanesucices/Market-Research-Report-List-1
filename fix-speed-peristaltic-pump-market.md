<p><p>The fix speed peristaltic pump market is primarily categorized into two types: peristaltic tube pumps and peristaltic hose pumps. Peristaltic tube pumps utilize flexible tubes to move fluids, making them ideal for applications requiring precise flow rates and minimal contamination. In contrast, peristaltic hose pumps employ hoses, offering higher durability and the ability to handle thicker or abrasive materials. Both pump types are widely used across various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and chemical processing.</p></p>
