Taillights signal a boat's position to vessels behind. The masthead light provides visibility from ahead, while all-round lights are visible from all directions, often used by anchored vessels. Additionally, other specialized lights cater to specific needs, ensuring compliance with maritime regulations and enhancing safety in diverse marine conditions.</p></p>
