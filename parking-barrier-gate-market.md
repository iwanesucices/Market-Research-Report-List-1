<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parking barrier gate market is primarily divided into two types: straight and crank gates. Straight barrier gates use a horizontal arm that swings vertically to allow vehicle access, making them efficient for high-traffic areas. In contrast, crank barrier gates utilize a rotating mechanism to lift or lower the gate arm, often seen in locations with limited space. Both types serve to enhance security and traffic management while catering to specific installation and operational needs in diverse environments.</p></p>
