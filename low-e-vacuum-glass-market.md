<p><p>The Low-E Vacuum Glass market consists of three primary types: Single Low-E Glass, Double Low-E Glass, and Triple Low-E Glass. Single Low-E Glass features a single glazing with a low-emissivity coating, enhancing thermal performance. Double Low-E Glass incorporates two glass panes separated by a vacuum, providing superior insulation and energy efficiency. Triple Low-E Glass features three panes, delivering maximum thermal resistance and noise reduction. Each type caters to varying energy efficiency needs, architectural designs, and climatic conditions.</p></p>
