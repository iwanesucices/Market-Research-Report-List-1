<p><strong>The Automotive CFRP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting</li><li>Thermoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive CFRP (Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer) market is primarily divided into two types: thermosetting and thermoplastic CFRP. Thermosetting CFRP involves resins that harden when cured and provide high strength and rigidity, making them ideal for structural components in vehicles. In contrast, thermoplastic CFRP uses resins that can be melted and reshaped, offering advantages in recycling and faster production cycles. Both types cater to the growing demand for lightweight materials in the automotive industry to enhance performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
