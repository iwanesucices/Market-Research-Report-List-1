<p><p>The vinyl glove market is divided into two main types: powdered and non-powdered gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with cornstarch or other substances to facilitate easy donning and reduce friction. However, they may pose allergy risks for some users. Non-powdered gloves, on the other hand, are free from these coatings, making them a safer choice for sensitive individuals, but they may be slightly more challenging to wear. Both types serve various applications in healthcare, food service, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
