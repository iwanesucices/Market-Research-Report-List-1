<p><p>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) Exposure Machine market offers advanced technologies for producing high-precision printed circuit boards (PCBs). The Polygon Mirror 365nm type employs a rotating polygon mirror to direct laser light, enabling fast imaging with excellent resolution suitable for intricate designs. In contrast, the DMD 405nm (Digital Micromirror Device) technology uses an array of tiny mirrors controlled digitally, allowing for precise patterning and high-quality imaging at shorter wavelengths, ideal for complex PCB applications requiring greater detail.</p></p>
