<p><ul><li>Photocatalytic Oxidation</li><li>Bi-polar Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ionic Air Purifier market includes technologies like Photocatalytic Oxidation (PCO) and Bi-polar Ionization. PCO utilizes ultraviolet light and a catalyst to convert pollutants into harmless substances, effectively breaking down harmful organic compounds. In contrast, Bi-polar Ionization generates positive and negative ions that attach to airborne particles, promoting agglomeration and removal of contaminants from the air. Both methods enhance indoor air quality by targeting different types of pollutants, catering to diverse consumer preferences in air purification technology.</p></p>
