<p><p>The Shrink Sleeve Labeling Equipment market is segmented into two primary types: vertical and horizontal shrink sleeve labeling systems. Vertical shrink sleeve labeling systems are designed for products that are taller than they are wide, using gravity to position items for labeling. In contrast, horizontal shrink sleeve labeling systems cater to wider packages, effectively labeling items as they move through the system horizontally. Both systems utilize heat to shrink the label tightly around the product, ensuring a secure and visually appealing finish.</p></p>
