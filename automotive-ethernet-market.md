<p><p>The Automotive Ethernet market encompasses various network types tailored for vehicle communication. Automotive Local Area Network (LAN) focuses on short-range connections within a vehicle, enabling high-speed data transfer between components such as sensors, infotainment systems, and driver assistance features. In contrast, Automotive Metropolitan Area Network (MAN) extends connectivity over larger geographical areas, facilitating communication between vehicles and external infrastructure, like traffic management systems and cloud services. Together, they enhance vehicle efficiency, safety, and the overall driving experience.</p></p>
