<p><p>Micro Invasive Glaucoma Surgery (MIGS) devices provide innovative solutions for managing glaucoma with minimal invasiveness. The market includes implants, such as stents and shunts, which are designed to help drain fluid from the eye and lower intraocular pressure effectively. Blink-assisted devices, on the other hand, enhance the natural blinking mechanism to facilitate aqueous humor outflow and improve eye health. Both categories aim to improve patient comfort, reduce recovery time, and effectively manage glaucoma symptoms with less risk compared to traditional surgery.</p></p>
