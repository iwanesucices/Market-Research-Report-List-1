<p><p>The pet syringe pump market includes single-channel and multi-channel syringe pumps. Single-channel syringe pumps are designed to deliver medication from one syringe at a time, offering simplicity and ease of use for pet care. In contrast, multi-channel syringe pumps can administer medications from multiple syringes simultaneously, allowing for more complex treatment regimens, particularly in veterinary clinics with multiple patients or for animals requiring various medications concurrently. Both types enhance precision and control in pet healthcare.</p></p>
