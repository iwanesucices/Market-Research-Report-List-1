<p><p>The Lightweight Insulating Castable market is primarily divided into two categories: Casting Type and Dry Gunning Type. Casting Type involves pouring a liquid mixture into molds, allowing for intricate shapes and uniformity in thickness, making it ideal for complex applications. In contrast, Dry Gunning Type utilizes a pneumatic gun to apply dry materials that are activated with water on-site, offering flexibility and ease of use in repair and maintenance tasks. Both types provide excellent thermal insulation and energy efficiency.</p></p>
