<p><p>The retarder market comprises two primary types: electric and hydraulic retarders. Electric retarders use electrical energy to generate resistance and slow down vehicles, offering precise control and efficiency, mainly in electric or hybrid vehicles. Hydraulic retarders, on the other hand, utilize fluid dynamics to dissipate energy and provide consistent braking in heavy trucks and buses. Both types enhance vehicle safety and performance, ensuring effective braking under various driving conditions while reducing wear on traditional brake components.</p></p>
