<p><p>The automotive splash guard market is divided into two main types: front splash guards and rear splash guards. Front splash guards are designed to protect the vehicle's lower body and wheel wells from road debris, dirt, and moisture, thereby enhancing durability and aesthetic appeal. Rear splash guards, meanwhile, focus on the rear section of the vehicle, preventing mud and water from being kicked up by the tires, which can help maintain cleanliness and prevent corrosion. Both types contribute significantly to vehicle longevity and performance.</p></p>
