<p><p>The Optical Time-Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) market is categorized into two primary types: Singlemode OTDR and Multimode OTDR. Singlemode OTDR is designed for long-distance fiber networks, utilizing a single light mode to provide high precision and minimal loss over extended ranges, making it ideal for telecommunications. In contrast, Multimode OTDR is used for shorter distances, supporting multiple light modes and offering faster data transmission in local area networks. Both types are essential for testing, troubleshooting, and maintaining fiber optic networks.</p></p>
