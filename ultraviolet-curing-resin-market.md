<p><strong>The Ultraviolet-Curing Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiation Curing</li><li>Cation Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) Curing Resin market encompasses two primary types: radiation curing and cation curing. Radiation curing utilizes UV light to initiate polymerization, making it ideal for fast-drying coatings, inks, and adhesives. This method offers rapid curing times and minimal solvent emissions. Cation curing, on the other hand, employs a chemical reaction involving cationic photoinitiators that allows for curing in the presence of moisture and can provide enhanced adhesion and chemical resistance, making it suitable for diverse applications in industries such as automotive and packaging.</p></p>
