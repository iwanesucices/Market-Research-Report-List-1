<p><p>The biosolids market is primarily categorized into two types: industrial wastewater and municipal wastewater. Industrial wastewater biosolids are generated from manufacturing processes and often contain specific contaminants, requiring specialized treatment and handling. In contrast, municipal wastewater biosolids stem from sewage treatment plants and consist of organic matter and nutrients, which can be recycled as fertilizers or soil amendments. Both markets focus on sustainable management practices to reduce environmental impact while maximizing resource recovery and beneficial reuse.</p></p>
