<p><strong>The Advanced Dermal Fillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Fillers</li><li>Non-absorbable Fillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The advanced dermal fillers market can be categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable fillers. Absorbable fillers, often made from hyaluronic acid or collagen, gradually break down in the body and typically last from six months to two years. They are popular for facial contouring and wrinkle reduction. Non-absorbable fillers, like polymethylmethacrylate (PMMA), provide longer-lasting results but are more permanent and can carry higher risks. Both types serve various aesthetic purposes, addressing volume loss and enhancing facial features.</p></p>
