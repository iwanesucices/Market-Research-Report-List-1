<p><ul><li>Bonded NdFeB Magnets</li><li>Sintered NdFeB Magnets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-performance NdFeB permanent magnets market is primarily categorized into two types: bonded NdFeB magnets and sintered NdFeB magnets. Bonded NdFeB magnets are produced by combining powdered materials with a polymer binder, offering flexibility and complex shapes, which are ideal for applications requiring precision and lightweight components. Sintered NdFeB magnets, on the other hand, are made by compressing and heating the powder, resulting in higher magnetic strength and density, making them suitable for demanding applications in industries like automotive and electronics.</p></p>
