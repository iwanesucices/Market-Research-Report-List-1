<p><p>Copper foil sheets and rolls are essential materials in various industries, primarily electronics. The market comprises two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through mechanical processes, offering high thickness uniformity and flexibility, making it ideal for applications like flexible printed circuits. Electrolytic copper foil, on the other hand, is created through an electroplating process, which results in thinner, high-quality foils suitable for advanced electronics and high-performance applications, such as batteries and circuit boards.</p></p>
