<p><p>Silicon-on-Insulator (SOI) technology involves the fabrication of silicon wafers with an insulating layer, typically silicon dioxide, that separates the top silicon layer from the bulk substrate. This architecture enhances performance in various electronic devices by reducing parasitic capacitance, improving speed, and lowering power consumption. SOI is widely used in applications such as radio frequency (RF) devices, microprocessors, andMEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems), making it crucial for the advancement of modern electronics.</p><p>The SOI market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-performance, energy-efficient devices in sectors like consumer electronics, telecommunications, and automotive. The emergence of 5G technology and advancements in edge computing are further propelling market expansion. Moreover, the push toward miniaturization in semiconductor devices is leading manufacturers to adopt SOI technology for its superior scalability.</p><p>The Silicon-on-Insulators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
