<p><ul><li>Independent Suspension Module</li><li>Semi-Dependent Suspension Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive rear suspension module market comprises two primary types: independent suspension modules and semi-dependent suspension modules. Independent suspension modules allow each wheel to move independently, enhancing ride comfort and handling performance. This design is commonly found in higher-end vehicles. In contrast, semi-dependent suspension modules link wheels together, providing a balance between comfort and cost-effectiveness, often used in mid-range vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle stability, handling, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
