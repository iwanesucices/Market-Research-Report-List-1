<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Column EPS，C-EPS</li><li>Pinion EPS，P-EPS</li><li>Rack EPS，R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile electric power steering (EPS) market comprises several types, each serving specific vehicle requirements. Column EPS integrates the motor within the steering column, providing a compact design. C-EPS involves a combination of column and rack systems, optimizing space and efficiency. Pinion EPS positions the motor at the pinion gear, enhancing responsiveness. P-EPS and Rack EPS utilize a motor connected to the rack for precise steering assistance. R-EPS combines various elements for improved performance and adaptability in modern vehicles.</p></p>
