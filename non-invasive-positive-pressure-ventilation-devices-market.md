<p><ul><li>CPAP Device</li><li>BiPAP Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Invasive Positive Pressure Ventilation (NIPPV) Devices market primarily includes Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices and Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP) devices. CPAP devices maintain a constant airflow to keep airways open, primarily used for obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, BiPAP devices offer varying pressures for inhalation and exhalation, providing more comfort for patients with conditions like chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). Both device types play crucial roles in managing respiratory conditions without invasive procedures.</p></p>
